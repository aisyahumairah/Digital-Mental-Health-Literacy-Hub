Tips for managing exam anxiety?

Have you tried the 5-4-3-2-1 grounding technique? When you feel anxious, identify 5 things you can see, 4 things you can touch, 3 things you can hear, 2 things you can smell, and 1 thing you can taste. It helps bring you back to the present moment.
